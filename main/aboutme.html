About me

My name is Angela Godfriedovna A. Twum and I am a junior astrophysicist from Ghana, West Africa.

I received my BA in Physics from Pomona College, CA in 2018 with a focus in Astrophysics.

In my research, I use computational and statistical methods to answer astrophysical questions pertaining to galaxy evolution, tidal disruption events and black holes.

Additionally, I have strong thirst for knowledge and a personal interest in the status of astronomy and technological development in Ghana and Africa.

I consider myself a curious scientist, artist, amateur programmer and life-long learner.

My interests include computer science, history, physiology, astrobiology, astrochemistry, latin dance, psychology, technology, art and design.

In my free time I enjoy engaging in activities that allow me to learn and be creative. I make time to teach myself new coding skills that add to my programming tool box and find value in self expression through art, music, and writing.
